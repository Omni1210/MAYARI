The Aerospace Engineering Students’ Society (AEROESS) stands as a collective of young innovators driven by curiosity, discipline, and the pursuit of flight. Rooted in the values of Batangas State University, the society empowers students to explore the frontiers of aerospace through collaboration, research, and hands-on learning. Guided by a shared vision of shaping tomorrow's skies, AEROESS cultivates leaders who are not only skilled in engineering but inspired to push boundaries and elevate the future of aerospace.

At the heart of the Aerospace Engineering Students’ Society (AEROESS) stands a dedicated team of mentors and student leaders committed to shaping the next generation of aerospace innovators. Guided by the organization’s Adviser, whose expertise provides direction and academic grounding, the council of student leaders drives the society’s initiatives with passion, discipline, and purpose. From executive officers to committee heads, each member brings a unique strength that fuels collaboration, empowers student growth, and upholds the organization’s vision. Together, they form a united leadership devoted to cultivating excellence, fostering innovation, and inspiring every aspiring engineer to soar toward their highest potential.

EXECUTIVE COUNCIL

The Executive Council of the Aeronautics Engineering Students' Society (AERSSOC) leads the organization in fulfilling the vision, and executing dynamic development initiatives. From the President who oversees, implements, and evaluates Student Development and Academic Management programs and facilities, and the other officers who administer specific units, relationships, and individuals within the Aeronautics Engineering student body, the AERSSOC officers work together to create alignment among the organization’s mission, vision, and values. The Executive Council also adopts specific processes, together with general planning and decision-making strategies to ensure the growth, consistency, and long-term success of the organization.
