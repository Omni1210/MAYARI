The Aerospace Engineering Students’ Society (AEROESS) stands as a collective of young innovators driven by curiosity, discipline, and the pursuit of flight. Rooted in the values of Batangas State University, the society empowers students to explore the frontiers of aerospace through collaboration, research, and hands-on learning.

Guided by a shared vision of shaping tomorrow's skies, AEROESS cultivates leaders who are not only skilled in engineering but inspired to push boundaries and elevate the future of aerospace.
