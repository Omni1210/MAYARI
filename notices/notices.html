NOVEMBER 23, 2025

OFFICIAL LAUNCH OF “PROJECT MAYARI”: AEROESS’ GATEWAY TO ACADEMIC PROFICIENCY AND CAREER PROFESSION BUILDING

The launch of Project Mayari marks a significant milestone in AEROESS’ commitment to advancing aerospace education and student development. Designed as a centralized digital hub, the new website houses learning resources, project showcases, research outputs, and opportunities for skill enhancement tailored for aspiring aerospace engineers and industry-aligned learners. Through Project Mayari, AEROESS aims to strengthen academic proficiency by offering structured learning pathways, technical articles, and multimedia materials that support both foundational understanding and specialized knowledge.

The platform also highlights the organization’s expanding involvement in national and international competitions, including interdisciplinary projects that integrate robotics, UAV systems, and space-related technologies. Beyond academic advancement, the initiative serves as a gateway for career and professional building. Students will gain access to mentorship programs, industry-linked training activities, and features that allow them to document their competencies, certifications, and project experience.

According to AEROESS, this digital ecosystem is expected to help students develop strong professional profiles that reflect both practical capability and innovation. The launch event was held in collaboration with the Project Orbital team, symbolizing the collective effort of members working toward the growth of the organization and the development of the next generation of aerospace leaders.
